🎾 1-Page Match Pocket Card¶

*Thẻ Túi Trận 1 Trang¶

How to use this card - Print on card stock (heavier paper survives sweat and bag wear) - Laminate for water resistance - Fold to fit your tennis bag pocket (the side pocket, not the racket compartment) - Read between games (during changeovers, not during points) - Glance at the V check before every serve

Cách dùng thẻ này - In trên giấy cứng - Ép plastic để chống nước - Gấp vừa túi bên túi tennis (túi bên, không phải ngăn vợt) - Đọc giữa các game (lúc nghỉ giải lao, không trong điểm) - Nhìn kiểm tra V trước mỗi serve

  ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ✓ WRIST    (no wrist snap, natural underspin from open face)
  ✓ ELBOW    (pronation, not wrist snap, no varus torque)
  ✓ SHOULDER (6-inch punch, not full swing)
  ✓ KNEE     (via 70% sprint on slice approach)

  THE 6 COMMON ERRORS  |  6 LỖI THƯỜNG GẶP
  ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  2. Western on 50+ body     (fix: 1 o'clock is the 50+ default)
  3. Continental on forehand (fix: V at 1 for forehand)
  4. Forget to change at net (fix: bevel walk before the net)
  5. Wrist snap for topspin  (fix: forearm rotation, not wrist)
  6. No 5-second reset       (fix: walk-look-check-walk)

  3. "V at 11 = Continental"       — The V is the steering wheel
  4. "Bevel walk in 1 second"      — 1-2 bevels from Continental
  5. "Forearm rotation, not wrist" — Joint-friendly topspin

  THE 50+ LONGEVITY RULE  |  QUY TẮC SỐNG THỌ 50+
  ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • The grip that RESPECTS the joints plays for 20+ years
  • The grip that LOADS the joints plays for 10 years
  • Continental = respects wrist, elbow, shoulder (4 protected)
  • Western = loads wrist, elbow, shoulder (4 at risk)
  • Semi-Western = respects joints (forearm rotation, not wrist)

   Continental is home base. Semi-Western is forehand. 5-second reset.

During the Match¶

Moment Tiếng Việt

After a good point 🇺🇸 Find the cue that fits (e.g., "Forearm rotation, not wrist") 🇻🇳 Tìm câu nhắc phù hợp (VD: "Xoay cẳng tay, không cổ tay")
