## Key 3.5-level choices I made in v2
- Added 3 new Parts instead of overstuffing existing Parts. Lob & Overhead, Slice Family, and Grip Pressure each got their own Part instead of being absorbed into Part 3 / Part 1. A 50+ player dephát bóngs dedicated spás for the vertical ván, the cắt family, and the pressure dial.
- Elevated Mental Game from Part 8 to Part 13. Mental Game was always important; in v1 it was a footnote. In v2 it's a full Part with the 5-phase trận map, the 4-step pre-điểm ritual, the 5 pressure điểms, and the 4-action loss recovery. The body leads the mind dephát bóngs a full chapter.
- 2-page cheat sheet instead of 1. 14 parts won't fit on one page. Page 1 covers Parts 1-7 (singles + foundation). Page 2 covers Parts 8-14 (doubles + lob + cắt + injury + pressure + mental + master cue).
- Stayed at the map level — ~90 KB, not 200 KB. The 22 deep dives have the detail; the manual has the index.
- Skipped frame counts and joint angles — those belong at 4.5+. The manual is for playing, not for academic study.
- Translated biomechanics into body cues — "gravity Phát Bóng" not "kinematic chain with peak angular velocity in distal segments."
- Made the 50+ adjustments pervasive — every part has a 50+ block. The 50+ reality is the filter for every cue.
- Embedded the 2-page cheat sheet at the end — fits the Surrey bag when folded.
- Cross-linked to ALL 22 deep dives + 4 foundational files — when the manual is too short, the source notes and deep dives are right there.
- Web-only output — no PDF pipeline. The HTML version is printable from any browser; no extra build step, no extra binary, no extra dependency to install.
- Prephát bóngd v1 unchanged — series parameters, Giọng nói, Part 8 mental section are all kept for archival continuity.
